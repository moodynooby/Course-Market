## 2025-05-15 - [Consistent Search Empty States]
**Learning:** Users benefit from a consistent way to recover from "no results" search states. Reusing a central 'EmptyState' component with a clear 'Reset/Clear' call-to-action reduces cognitive load and maintains visual harmony across the application.
**Action:** Always use the 'EmptyState' component for empty search or list views, and ensure a 'Clear' button is available both in the search field and as an action in the empty state.
